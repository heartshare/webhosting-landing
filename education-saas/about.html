Our Story

EduFlow started in 2020 with a simple observation: traditional online courses weren't working. Learners were dropping out, feeling isolated, and struggling to stay motivated. The one-size-fits-all approach was failing.

Our founders, James and Sarah, met while working on a research project at MIT. They realized that AI could solve this problem by personalizing the learning experience — adapting content, pace, and recommendations to each individual.

Today, EduFlow serves over 100,000 learners worldwide. We've built a platform that doesn't just deliver content but actively guides each student on their unique learning journey. And we're just getting started.
