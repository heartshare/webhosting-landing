Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions

How do I reset my password?

Click "Forgot Password" on the login page and enter your email address. We'll send you a link to reset your password.

Can I get a refund?

We offer a 14-day free trial for all new users. If you cancel during the trial period, you won't be charged. After that, refunds are handled on a case-by-case basis.

How do I access my courses?

After logging in, go to the "My Courses" section in your dashboard. All your enrolled courses will be listed there with your progress.

Do you offer certificates?

Yes! Upon completing a course, you'll receive a certificate of completion that you can share on LinkedIn or include in your resume.
