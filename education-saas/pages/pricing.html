Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about pricing

Can I cancel my subscription anytime?

Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time. Your access will continue until the end of your billing period.

Do you offer refunds?

We offer a 14-day free trial so you can try before you buy. If you're not satisfied, cancel within the trial period at no cost.

What's included in the Pro plan?

Pro includes access to all courses, AI learning paths, certificates, priority support, and offline access to content.

Can I switch plans later?

Absolutely! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time from your account settings.
